Available Certificates

🏛

Barangay Clearance

General-purpose clearance certifying that you have no pending cases or disputes within the barangay. Required for employment, business permits, and government transactions.

Requirements
  • Valid Government ID
  • Proof of Residency
  • ₱50 Processing Fee
🏠︎

Certificate of Residency

Certifies that you are a bona fide resident of BayanLink. Commonly required for school enrollment, scholarship applications, and bank transactions.

Requirements
  • Valid Government ID
  • Utility Bill (same address)
  • ₱30 Processing Fee
❤

Certificate of Indigency

Certifies that you belong to a low-income household. Required for free medical assistance, Philhealth benefits, and legal aid from the Public Attorney's Office (PAO).

Requirements
  • Valid Government ID
  • 4Ps or DSWD Certificate (if any)
  • Free of Charge
✉

Business Clearance

Required for small businesses and sari-sari stores operating within the barangay. Precondition for obtaining a Mayor's Business Permit from the municipal office.

Requirements
  • Owner's Valid ID
  • DTI / SEC Registration
  • ₱150 Processing Fee
☻

Barangay ID

Official barangay identification card valid for all local transactions. Especially useful for senior citizens, students, and residents without a national ID.

Requirements
  • 1x1 or 2x2 Photo
  • Birth Certificate
  • ₱20 ID Fee
𓀗

Senior Citizen Certification

Verifies senior citizen status for government benefits, discounts under the Expanded Senior Citizens Act, and priority lane access in government offices.

Requirements
  • Birth Certificate or Valid ID
  • OSCA Registration (if available)
  • Free of Charge

ℹ  Office hours: Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM  |  Closed on Holidays

Free Medical Mission — June 15, 2026

The barangay health center, in partnership with Cavite Provincial Health Office, will conduct a free medical mission. Services include general checkup, blood pressure monitoring, blood sugar testing, and medicine distribution for hypertension and diabetes. All residents are welcome. Bring your barangay ID or valid ID. Mission starts at 8:00 AM at the Barangay Covered Court.

General

Scheduled Water Interruption — June 14

The Indang Water District will conduct maintenance work on the main water pipeline serving Zone 3 (Sitio Maliwanag and Sitio Pag-asa). Water supply will be interrupted from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM on June 14, 2026. Please store enough water for the day. We apologize for the inconvenience.

07
Jun
Urgent

Dengue Advisory — Increased Cases Reported

The Barangay Health Center has recorded a 30% increase in dengue cases this month. Residents are reminded to: eliminate all stagnant water around the household, use mosquito nets especially for children, apply mosquito repellent, and immediately consult the health center for fever lasting more than 2 days. The barangay will conduct fogging operations on June 12 and 13.

Jun

BayanLink Fiesta 2026 — June 25–27

The Annual Barangay Fiesta is happening on June 25–27, 2026! Celebrate with us: street parade on the 25th, cultural show and agri-fair on the 26th, and a basketball tournament on the 27th. All residents and guests are welcome. Venue: Barangay Plaza and Covered Court.

Curfew Reminder for Minors

In accordance with Ordinance No. 2023-04, minors under 18 years old are prohibited from loitering in public places without parental supervision after 10:00 PM. Parents and guardians are urged to monitor their children. Violations may result in the minor being brought to the Barangay Hall for custody.

Rabies Vaccination Drive for Pets

The Bureau of Animal Industry (BAI) will conduct a free anti-rabies vaccination for dogs and cats on May 28, 2026, 8:00 AM–12:00 PM, at the Barangay Hall grounds. Pet owners are required to bring their animals on a leash or in a carrier. This is free and open to all barangay residents.
